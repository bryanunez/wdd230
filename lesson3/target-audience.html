Target Audience

Description

Our target audience are businesses or individuals who are interested in networking with other leaders or businesses in the area to improve the tourism in the region.

Personas

Persona 1
Persona: Public Relations Manager
Fictional Name: Félix Flores.
Job title / Responsabilities: Public Relations Manager / Manage the chambers's communication initiatives with media personnel
Demographics: 33 years old, married, father of one children, has a master in public relations.
Goals and Tasks: Deliver Press releases and create promotional material.
Environment: Has an understanding of media and has public communication skills.
Inhibiting Factors: Staying up-to-date with the audience and their concerns.
Quote: "Península Chamber of Commerce is working to build up relations with bussines owners to improve the tourism in region and growth opportunities for local bussinesses."
Persona 2
Persona: Local Bussiness Owner
Fictional Name: Mahonri Garrido.
Job title / Responsabilities: Entrepreneur / Serve customers, manage employees and day to day operations.
Demographics: 30 years old, single, no children, has a BS in Business Management.
Goals and Tasks: Grow the business, increase the profit.
Environment: Uses computer and bussines management tools, uses email and web searches extensively.
Inhibiting Factors: Investing in the business
Quote: "You can't make an omelette without breaking some eggs."

Scenarios
